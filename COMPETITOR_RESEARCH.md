# Competitor Research: Swiss Web Design & SEO Market

## Primary Competitor: OnlineOptimierung.ch

### Overview
- **Location**: Basel, Switzerland
- **Team**: 3 people (Mauro - Account Manager, Maria - SEO/SEA, Patrick - Design/Support)
- **Founded**: ~2024 (Webflow site, recent branding)
- **Claimed stats**: 186+ projects, 87M+ CHF revenue generated for clients, 4.9/5 (59 reviews)

### Services
- Web Design (Webflow-based)
- SEO
- Google Ads / SEA
- Online Shops (Shopify)
- Adobe Commerce
- "My Control" (proprietary dashboard)

### Strengths
1. **Strong social proof** - 59 Google reviews at 4.9/5 is substantial
2. **Local SEO pages** - Targeting Zürich, Basel, St. Gallen, Baden, Aarau with dedicated landing pages
3. **Case studies** - Detailed before/after for Cristofoli AG, René Ruepp Orthopädie, HairIn Clinic
4. **Pain-point messaging** - Strong copy around "your website wins no customers", "Google Ads burn money"
5. **Free discovery call** - Calendly integration for booking
6. **Bilingual** - German-focused with professional copy

### Weaknesses (Your Opportunities)
1. **Zero pricing transparency** - Every price is "auf Anfrage". This creates friction and distrust.
2. **Slow, bloated website** - Webflow site with ~15MB page weight, 150+ requests. Bad for SEO irony.
3. **Generic template aesthetic** - Looks like a Webflow template. No unique brand identity.
4. **Account manager layer** - Mauro is an intermediary. Clients don't talk directly to the doer.
5. **Broad generalist** - Tries to do webdesign + SEO + Google Ads + Shopify + Adobe Commerce. Jack of all trades.
6. **No performance guarantees** - Claims "6x conversions" but no refund guarantee.
7. **Webflow lock-in** - Clients stuck on Webflow ecosystem, monthly fees, limited flexibility.
8. **Stock photo heavy** - Generic corporate imagery, no real team photos or authentic content.

## Market Gaps & Your Differentiation (buweb.ch)

### 1. Transparent Pricing (Biggest Win)
**The gap**: OnlineOptimierung.ch hides all pricing. Beyondweb only shows "from" prices. Most agencies force a call.

**Your move**: Keep the CHF 3,900 fixed price prominently displayed. Add a simple pricing page with clear tiers. Swiss business owners hate "Verkaufsgespräche" where they don't know the price.

### 2. Performance as Proof
**The gap**: OO's own site is slow Webflow bloat. Beyondweb uses WordPress. None can claim sub-1s load times.

**Your move**: Lead with Core Web Vitals scores. "Your site loads in 0.3s. Theirs loads in 4.2s. Google rewards speed." Show Lighthouse 100 scores.

### 3. Direct Developer Contact
**The gap**: OO has Mauro (account manager) as intermediary. Big agencies have junior teams.

**Your move**: "You talk to me. I write the code. No account managers, no juniors." This is your freelancer advantage.

### 4. Specialization Over Generalization
**The gap**: OO does webdesign + SEO + Google Ads + Shopify + Adobe Commerce. They can't be world-class at all of them.

**Your move**: Position as "SEO-first web development." Don't offer Google Ads management. Don't do Shopify. Be the best at SEO + fast websites. Refer out for ads.

### 5. The 120-Day Guarantee
**The gap**: No competitor offers a money-back guarantee on rankings.

**Your move**: This is your killer USP. Lead with it. "Page 1 in 120 days or 100% refund." OO claims "6x conversions" but offers zero risk reversal.

### 6. No Lock-In / Open Source
**The gap**: OO uses Webflow (proprietary, monthly fees). Beyondweb uses WordPress (plugin dependencies).

**Your move**: Astro static sites = zero dependencies. Client owns everything. No monthly platform fees. "Your site works forever, even if you fire me."

### 7. Technical Differentiation
**The gap**: All competitors use page builders or CMSs.

**Your move**: "Hand-coded Astro sites" - mention Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind. This signals technical competence that page-builder agencies can't match.

## Tactical Recommendations

### 1. Add a Comparison Section
Create a dedicated "Why buweb.ch vs agencies" section that transparently compares against OnlineOptimierung.ch (without naming them explicitly):
- "Agencies hide pricing behind calls" vs "Fixed CHF 3,900"
- "Agencies use page builders" vs "Hand-coded Astro"
- "Agencies have account managers" vs "Direct developer contact"

### 2. Speed as Lead Magnet
Offer a free Core Web Vitals report (not just SEO audit). Most Swiss business owners have never seen their Lighthouse score. This is more tangible than SEO audits.

### 3. Local SEO Domination
OO has pages for Basel, Zürich, St. Gallen, Baden, Aarau. You should too. But go deeper:
- Industry × City combinations ("Webdesign für Zahnärzte Basel")
- Canton-specific content
- German + French versions for Romandie

### 4. Pricing Page
Create `/preise` or `/pricing` with:
- Business Pro: CHF 3,900 (fixed)
- Add-ons: Blog setup, multilingual, advanced schema
- Monthly retainer: CHF 790/month
- Show what's included vs what's extra

### 5. Address the "Too Cheap" Perception
At CHF 3,900, you're cheaper than Beyondweb but more expensive than Winwebdesign. Position this intentionally:
- "Not the cheapest. Not the most expensive. The best value."
- "CHF 3,900 is what a single Google Ads month costs at an agency. I build you an asset that works forever."
