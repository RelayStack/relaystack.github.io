## What's Wrong With Your Current Pricing

### Problem 1: Missing Middle Tier
You have only 2 tiers: FREE and CHF 3,900. This is a massive gap. Most buyers need 3 options:
- **Low** (anchor / starter)
- **Middle** (most popular / recommended)
- **High** (premium / full service)

Without a middle tier, CHF 3,900 feels like a big leap from FREE.

### Problem 2: The CHF 3,900 "Business Pro" Is Ambiguous
It includes "website (5-7 pages)" + "complete SEO foundation" + "1 month support". But what if someone ALREADY has a website? They might not want to pay CHF 3,900 for a new site.

You need to split into:
- **Website + SEO** (for those who need both)
- **SEO Only** (for those with an existing site)

### Problem 3: No "Quick Win" Paid Option
Between FREE (audit) and CHF 3,900 (full project), there's nothing. A CHF 500-1,000 "Quick Fix" or "Google Business Profile optimization" would capture leads who aren't ready for a full project.

### Problem 4: The Retainer Is Vague
"Monthly SEO retainers from CHF 790/month" - what does that include? How many hours? What's the deliverable?

### Problem 5: Payment Plan Buried
"or CHF 390/month x 10" is hidden in the note. This should be more prominent - it's a huge selling point for Swiss SMEs.

#### Tier 0: Free Lead Magnet (KEEP)
**SEO Audit** - CHF 0
- Complete technical SEO audit
- 3 quick-win recommendations
- 15-min strategy call
- Competitor analysis

#### Tier 1: Quick Win (NEW)
**Local SEO Boost** - CHF 990 (one-time)
- Google Business Profile optimization
- 5 directory submissions (local.ch, Google, etc.)
- NAP consistency check
- 2 review response templates
- 30-day check-in

*Why: Captures businesses not ready for a full website. Directly competes with Ubertiger's CHF 1,000 GBP service.*

#### Tier 2: The Website Package (REBRAND)
**Business Website** - CHF 3,900 (one-time)
- Professional website (5-7 pages)
- Mobile-first + speed optimized
- Conversion forms + tracking
- Schema markup + GBP setup
- 1 month support
- *Optional: Add SEO foundation for +CHF 1,500*

*Why: Split the website from the SEO. Some people just need a site.*

#### Tier 3: The Full Package (CURRENT "Business Pro")
**SEO + Website Bundle** - CHF 4,900 (one-time)
- Everything in Business Website
- Complete SEO foundation
- Keyword research + content briefs
- 3 months support
- 120-day page 1 guarantee
- *or CHF 490/month x 10*

*Why: This is your flagship. CHF 4,900 is still well below Beyondweb's CHF 4,900 (which is just a website).*

#### Tier 4: Ongoing (REFINE)
**Growth Retainer** - CHF 790/month (no minimum)
- 8 hours of SEO work/month
- Monthly performance report
- Content optimization (2 pages/month)
- Technical monitoring

- *No minimum contract - cancel anytime*

*Why: "No minimum contract" is a HUGE differentiator. SEO-Friends requires 3 months. Ubertiger requires ongoing. You don't.*

### Add-Ons (NEW)

## Customer Acquisition: 12 Tactics to Get More Clients

### Immediate (This Week)

**1. Google Business Profile Domination**
- Your GBP must be PERFECT before you sell it to others
- Post weekly updates on your GBP
- Add photos of your workspace, yourself, before/after screenshots
- Target keywords in your GBP description: "SEO Spezialist Basel", "Webdesign Basel"
- Get 5+ reviews ASAP (ask friends, family, past clients)

**2. Local SEO Pages**
Create dedicated landing pages for every city + service combination:
- `/seo-basel/`, `/seo-zuerich/`, `/seo-bern/`
- `/webdesign-basel/`, `/webdesign-zuerich/`
- `/google-business-profile-optimierung-basel/`

These rank for "[service] [city]" searches. OnlineOptimierung.ch does this - copy their strategy but do it better.

**3. Directory Listings (Free)**
Register on every Swiss business directory:
- local.ch
- search.ch
- gelbeseiten.ch
- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places
- Apple Business Connect
- LinkedIn Company Page (you have this?)
- XING (German-speaking professionals)

### Short-Term (This Month)

**4. Referral Program**
"Refer a business owner who becomes a client, get CHF 500 credit or cash."
- Your best leads come from referrals
- Make it explicit and easy
- Send a simple email to your 50+ past clients

**5. LinkedIn Content Strategy**
Post 3x per week:
- Monday: Before/after ranking screenshots (proof)
- Wednesday: "Myth vs Reality" about SEO (education)
- Friday: Quick tip that takes 5 minutes (value)

Example post: "This Basel restaurant was invisible on Google. 3 changes, 6 weeks, page 1. Here's exactly what I did: [screenshot]"

**6. Cold Outreach (Targeted)**
Find 20 businesses per week that:
- Have a website that loads slowly (test with PageSpeed Insights)
- Are on page 2+ for their main keyword
- Are in Basel/Zurich/Bern
- Have CHF 5M+ revenue (can afford you)

Send a 3-sentence email:
> "Hi [Name],
> I noticed [Company]'s website takes 4.2 seconds to load. Google penalizes slow sites - you're likely losing ranking positions because of this.
> I help Basel businesses fix this and get to page 1. Interested in a free 15-min analysis?
> [Your name]"

**7. Partner with Web Designers Who Don't Do SEO**
Many web designers (like Webartistik.ch) build sites but don't optimize them.
- Offer them CHF 500 referral fee per client
- They send you clients who need SEO
- You send them clients who need a redesign

### Medium-Term (3 Months)

**8. Industry-Specific Case Studies**
You have industry pages (restaurants, lawyers, trades). For each:
- Build one real client case study
- Include: Before ranking, after ranking, traffic increase, revenue impact
- Turn into a blog post + PDF download
- Gate behind email capture

**9. Free Tool / Calculator**
Build a simple tool on your site:
- "SEO ROI Calculator" - input monthly revenue, see what page 1 is worth
- "Website Speed Test" - check their site, show problems
- "Google Visibility Score" - simple 1-10 score

Tools get shared and linked to. Links = rankings.

**10. Guest Posts on Swiss Business Blogs**
Write for:
- KMU-Magazin
- Handelszeitung
- Swiss Startups
- Any Basel/Zurich business publication

Topic: "5 SEO Mistakes Swiss SMEs Make" - always end with a soft mention of your services.

**11. Local Networking**
- Basel Chamber of Commerce (Handelskammer)
- Industry associations (restaurants, lawyers, trades)
- Meetups and business events
- Coworking spaces (impactHub Basel, etc.)

Bring business cards. Your pitch: "I get Swiss businesses to Google page 1 in 120 days, guaranteed."

**12. Paid Ads (When Ready)**
- Google Ads: Bid on "SEO Agentur Basel", "Webdesign Basel"
- LinkedIn Ads: Target business owners in Basel/Zurich, company size 1-50
- Start with CHF 500/month budget
- Landing page must match the ad exactly
